# 🇧🇩 Sonar Bangladesh

> A cinematic digital encyclopedia dedicated to the landscapes, history, culture, heritage, and people of Bangladesh.

**Live Demo:** https://shonar-bangladesh.vercel.app

---

## Overview

**Sonar Bangladesh** is a modern, immersive web experience that presents Bangladesh as a living encyclopedia rather than a traditional informational website.

The project combines editorial storytelling, cinematic visuals, bilingual content (English & বাংলা), and interactive sections to celebrate the country's geography, history, rivers, heritage, festivals, and culture.

Designed with a clean editorial aesthetic, the website aims to make discovering Bangladesh engaging, educational, and visually memorable.

## Vision

Bangladesh is often introduced through statistics.

This project takes a different approach.

**Sonar Bangladesh** tells the story of a nation through its rivers, landscapes, language, architecture, traditions, and people—creating an experience that feels closer to exploring a beautifully designed encyclopedia than browsing a typical website.
